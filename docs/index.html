Lossless compression for satellite housekeeping telemetry data.

The definitive implementation of the CCSDS 124.0-B-1 POCKET+ lossless compression algorithm of fixed-length housekeeping data.


POCKET+ is patented by the European Space Agency (ESA), standardized by the Consultative Committee for Space Data Systems (CCSDS), and implemented by georges.fyi at Tanagra Space.
